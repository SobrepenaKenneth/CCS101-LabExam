About Kape-pindot ☕

Welcome to Kape-Pindot, an online pastry and coffee shop created as part of a school project. Our website was designed to showcase creativity, teamwork, and web development skills and is inspired by our love for coffee, sweets, and cozy café vibes. Through Kape-Pindot, we aim to give users a fun and relaxing online café experience where they can explore different coffee and pastry combinations. While this is not a real business, it reflects our passion for both design and food culture.
